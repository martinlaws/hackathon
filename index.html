What Is Hack With IX?

We’re bringing together some of Canada’s brightest young minds across multiple disciplines to bring bold ideas to the world of advertising analytics.
We want to empower students to show the ad tech world what they're capable of. #HackWithIX is an opportunity for students across multiple disciplines to work together to build the advertising analytics tools of the future.
This is an opportunity to learn from leading industry experts on topics including: ReactJS and Design Thinking. Ready to start your journey to becoming an ad tech leader of tomorrow?

What is The Goal?

Hack IX is Canada’s first ad tech hackathon. You’ll be surrounded by industry leaders, there will be lots of food and you’ll have the chance to win some amazing prizes so don’t miss it!
Students from across Canada are being brought together at the University of Toronto by Index Exchange to innovate, connect and create in 48 hours. We’re collecting hackers from all backgrounds and disciplines in one place to build an environment of radical
innovation where you can learn and create something that might disrupt an industry. Bring yourself, your creativity and an open mind because you’re about to embark on a journey you’ll never forget. As for the details - don’t sweat it - we’ve got them covered.

What Can You Win?

Index Exchange believes in rewarding hard work. That’s why each member of the winning team is going to take home an iPhone 7. Additionally, the second and third place teams will also be taking home prizes yet to be announced.

But remember, winning isn’t everything. You’ll have the opportunity to play with bleeding edge industry technology, grow your skills, and get mentorship from top talent.
